The village of Webberbridge has an active fundraising committee.

Mrs Weatherington is the chair of the committee and she is proud of the contribution everyone has made.

Money has been raised through all sorts of events over the years, including tombolas, welly wanging competitions and tugs of war.

A recent injection of money raised by the committee and funding provided by Sport England enabled an old barn, which was donated by a retired farmer David Plhough-Farrows, into a multipurpose sports hall.

David lived in Webberbridge all his life and played cricket, football and darts for the village team. Dave is aslo the inaugural winner of the Webberbridge Bog-Snorkelling challenge, which is run according to the Irish Bog-Snorkelling Association Rules.

In addition to the indoor activities the hall is the base for a number of other sports clubs including Golf and Bog Snorkelling.
